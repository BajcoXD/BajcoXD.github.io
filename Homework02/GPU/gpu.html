NVIDIA and AMD are two leading brands in the graphics processing unit (GPU) market, each offering unique strengths. NVIDIA is known for its advanced ray tracing capabilities and DLSS (Deep Learning Super Sampling), which enhance performance and visuals in high-resolution gaming. Their GPUs tend to excel in tasks like 3D rendering, AI workloads, and professional video editing, due to their CUDA cores and AI integration. AMD GPUs, on the other hand, provide solid gaming performance with their RDNA architecture and competitive pricing, often delivering better value for money, especially at midrange levels.
